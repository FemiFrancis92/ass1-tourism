About Us

Kerala, the land of Gods, is one of the best Indian states that attract a lot of travellers and tourists across the world. The state is known for its rich culture and heritage. Along with these, Kerala is one of those states where you get a lot of ethnic and authentic cuisines.Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Inland are the Western Ghats, mountains whose slopes support tea, coffee and spice plantations as well as wildlife. National parks like Eravikulam and Periyar, plus Wayanad and other sanctuaries, are home to elephants, langur monkeys and tigers.
